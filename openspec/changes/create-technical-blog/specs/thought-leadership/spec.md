## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Thought leadership demonstration

Blog posts MUST demonstrate expertise beyond basic tutorials, showing unique insights and experiences.

#### Scenario: Share unique perspective
- **WHEN** a blog post is written
- **THEN** it MUST provide insights not found in documentation
- **AND** MUST include lessons from real-world projects
- **AND** MUST demonstrate critical thinking about trade-offs

#### Scenario: Take position on debatable topics
- **WHEN** post covers controversial or debated topic
- **THEN** author MUST state clear position with justification
- **AND** MUST acknowledge alternative viewpoints
- **AND** MUST support position with evidence

### Requirement: Career trajectory visibility

Posts MUST demonstrate progression from analyst to senior data scientist, showing growth over time.

#### Scenario: Reference experience timeline
- **WHEN** post discusses real-world project
- **THEN** it MUST specify when project occurred (year, role)
- **AND** MUST show how approach evolved over time
- **AND** MUST connect past experiences to current expertise

#### Scenario: Demonstrate increasing responsibility
- **WHEN** post covers leadership topics
- **THEN** it MUST mention mentoring, cross-functional impact
- **AND** MUST show scale of projects (team size, business impact)
- **AND** MUST reflect senior-level perspective

### Requirement: Industry contribution

Posts MUST contribute to broader data science community knowledge, not just self-promotion.

#### Scenario: Share reusable frameworks
- **WHEN** post introduces framework or checklist
- **THEN** it MUST be generalizable beyond author's specific context
- **AND** MUST be actionable for readers
- **AND** MUST encourage adoption and feedback

#### Scenario: Engage with community
- **WHEN** post is published
- **THEN** author MUST share on LinkedIn, Twitter, relevant subreddits
- **AND** MUST encourage comments and discussion
- **AND** MUST respond to questions and feedback
