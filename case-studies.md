## Project Insights

Each case study follows a comprehensive 7-section framework:

1. **Executive Summary** (150 words) — Problem, solution, and impact at a glance
2. **Business Challenge** — Stakeholder pain points, success metrics, constraints
3. **Technical Approach** — Architecture diagrams (Mermaid), technology choices, data pipeline
4. **Implementation Journey** — Timeline, challenges faced, solutions applied
5. **Results & Impact** — Quantified business metrics using XYZ formula
6. **Lessons Learned** — What worked, what to do differently, applicability
7. **Future Roadmap** — Planned improvements, scaling opportunities
